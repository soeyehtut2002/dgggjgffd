Taekwondo

Taekwondo is a Korean martial art that focuses on
high-velocity, powerful kicks, precise hand techniques, and
a strong emphasis on mental fortitude.
It's known for its stunning acrobatic kicks and is an
excellent choice for those seeking both self-defense
skills and personal growth.

Judo

Judo, which means "gentle way" in Japanese, is a traditional martial art that originated in Japan. It places a strong emphasis on using an opponent's force against them through throws, pins, joint locks, and strangles. Judo is a unique discipline that combines physical prowess with mental discipline.

Kick Boxing

Kickboxing is a full-contact combat sport that draws
sport that draws its roots from various martial arts,
including Muay Thai, Karate, and Boxing.
It involves a combination of punches, kicks, and
knee strikes, making it a high-energy, calorie-burning,
and exciting form of exercise.

Lethwei

Lethwei, also known as Burmese bare-knuckle boxing,is a
traditional martial art and combat sport originating in Myanmar

demanding striking disciplines in the world, focusing on the use
of fists, elbows, knees, and shins.
